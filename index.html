Our Story

Portage's Favorite
Neighborhood Bar

RJ's Sports Bar & Grill has been a cornerstone of Portage for over half a century — the kind of place where the bartender knows your name, the burgers are stacked high, and the cold beer never runs out.

Catch the game on one of our many big screens, shoot a game of pool, relax on the back patio with a game of cornhole, or just grab a bite after work. RJ's is where southwest Michigan unwinds.
